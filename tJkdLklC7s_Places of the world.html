5 Famous Places in the world

1. The Tower Bridge,London,England

The Tower Bridge is an old bridge because it was built in 1886-1894 and it was directed by 3 people and it crosses the River Thames. Some engineers modified it to look to it's very best. England is a good country to visit or to live in because this is one of England's tourist attraction.The bridge was opened by Edward, Prince of Wales and Alexandra, Princess of Wales in 1894.

After the dedication it means a welcome to immagrants or visitors to the US

3. Taj Mahal,Agra,India

Taj Mahal was built in 1643 and Emperor Shah Jahan Owned it until he died in 1658 and it was a tomb for him and his wife. The tomb is the centrepiece of a 17-hectare (42-acre) complex, which includes a mosque and a guest house, and is set in formal gardens bounded on three sides by a crenellated wall.

4. Effiel Tower,Paris,France

The Effiel Tower was built from 1887-1889. The Effiel Tower Got It's Name From an engineer called Gustave Effiel as he was part of the company that built the tower The tower received 5,889,000 visitors in 2022, up by 197 per cent from 2021, when numbers dropped due to the COVID virus.The Eiffel Tower is the most visited monument with an entrance fee in the world.

Christ the Redeemer,Rio De Janerio,Brazil

Christ The Redeemer was built in 1922-1931.The Statue was created by French sculptor Paul Landowski and built by Brazilian engineer Heitor da Silva Costa, in collaboration with French engineer Albert Caquot. Romanian sculptor Gheorghe Leonida sculpted the face.Christ The Redeemer differs considerably from its original design, as the initial plan was a large Christ with a globe in one hand and a cross in the other.
